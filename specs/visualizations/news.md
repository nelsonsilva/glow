# RSS News Ticker

Scrolling news headlines from RSS feeds.

## Visual Output

Each article displays: thumbnail (left), source logo (top-right), date and time (center), and a horizontally scrolling summary/headline (bottom). After a headline finishes scrolling, advances to the next article.

On each run, one feed is randomly selected from 10 configured sources. Article thumbnails are pre-fetched before the render loop starts.

## Parameters

None.

## Frame Rate

30fps.
